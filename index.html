The Tang dynasty was initially ruled by Emperor Gaozu (Li Yuan) and his son, Emperor Taizong (Li Shimin). Emperor Gaozu

: Emperor gaozu was the founder of the Tang dynasty. He was originally a senior minister of the previous Sui dynasty who rose to power during its collapse. He was then crowned as emperor with his son Emperor TaiZhong. Emperor gaozu reigned from 618 to 626 during which his reign, Emperor gaozu defeated rival warlords, effectively trying to unify China under one empire. He adopted some laws and policies created by the first emperor of the Sui dynasty. He abolished the harsh laws and corrupt judicial system of the Sui Dynasty, lowered taxes, and implemented policies for the fair distribution of land, which helped stabilize the country and promote economic prosperity.
