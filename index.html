Republic of China (ROC)

The Republic of China was founded on January 1, 1912, after the fall of the Qing dynasty. Initially ruled by the Kuomintang (KMT), it unified the country and governed from Nanjing. After losing the civil war in 1949, the ROC government relocated to Taiwan, where it continues to exist today.
